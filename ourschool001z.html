School History

Gilharder is a school founded on the pretext of giving back to the society out of love. Our motto of - 'Owe no man anything, but to love one another.' from Romans 13:8. Gilharder has been a center that extends love to humankind in accordance with this Word. In the goal of transforming the grown man we eyed him from his childhood and have occupied that space to nurture informed adulthood by transmitting knowledge and faith to them. .
